Members

SPA has over 30 members. We represent many different majors and activites on campus. We have varsity atheletes competing on the volleyball, track, and baseball teams. Last year, we used our dorm-allocated activity fund to go play skyzone dodgeball!


Mafia/Gatas

After Simmons study breaks many of our members play Gatas which is a speedy billards game. In addition, we have intense games of mafia, play PS3 and Wii games, host a minecraft server on one of our over 20 home-built servers, and play Kerbal Space Program.


Dock Runs

If you head to the basement of Stata, chances are you will find one of our members looking for old computer parts, vaccuum pumps, or winches ("the crane"). We are always building cool projects and plan on hosting SPA hackathons!
